# About Sentimental-Analysis: Tesla Upcoming Cybertruck Project

The unicorn product of Tesla, Cybertruck, is without doubt one of the most eccentric-designed, versatile one among its own kind. However, there is not a small number of people find its design to be unaesthetic, and come with unneccessary additions. This sentiment-analysis is aiming to find out how welcoming the public are to the Cybertruck, and what kinds of criticisms are the most popular right now. So that, we could assess the impact the Cybertruck will make on the industry.
